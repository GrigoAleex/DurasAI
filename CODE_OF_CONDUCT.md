# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

We want DurasAI to be a respectful, high-signal collaboration space.

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, and discussions.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Keep communication relevant and professional.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, flamebait, or repeated bad-faith interactions.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Intentionally introducing malicious or breaking changes.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action needed to protect the project, including:

- Removing comments or content.
- Closing issues/PRs.
- Temporarily or permanently restricting participation.

Intentional breaking or malicious contributions can result in immediate exclusion from future development.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies across all project spaces, including GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and reviews.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer:

- GitHub: https://github.com/GrigoAleex

For sensitive reports, share minimal public details and request a private channel.
